### Decisions

- **Decision:** Shared read store lives in `MeterBarShared` as `SharedMetricsStore`, not a
  renamed `StorageKeys`.
  - **Context:** The app already has an app-only `StorageKeys` (Claude account keys, etc.).
  - **Rationale:** Avoids a name collision when the app imports `MeterBarShared`; keeps the
    app's UserDefaults keys out of the cross-target shared surface.
- **Decision:** `cost --days` reports only what daily rows carry (input/output/cache-read +
  cost), not cache-creation tokens or session counts.
  - **Context:** `DailyTokenUsage` doesn't store those; the dated data is the daily rows.
  - **Rationale:** Honest, rescan-free windowing; a truncation notice fires when the cache
    spans fewer days than requested rather than silently under-reporting.
